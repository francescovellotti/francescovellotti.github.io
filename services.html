Translation

Translation is my main skill, since I've studied languages from the age of 8 for my entire educational life. I've started with English and then I was introduced to German and French and I began to develop my translation skills in Middle School. At University, translation became my main skill.
Since then, I worked for different companies which required my services and I also developed a good knowledge of the main CAT tools.

Web Development

Web Development is my most recent accomplishment. I've always studied and worked in the humanistic field and then I decided to get some deeper knowledge of the IT environment. Don't get me wrong: I was always very "geeky", being into computers and videogames, but not to the point of having web development among my skills. This changed recently thanks to Start2Impact.
I have a good knowledge of pretty much everything regarding Front-End Development, such as the most recent versions of HTML, CSS and Bootstrap while my knowledge of JavaScript is improving greatly.

Copywriting

I have developed my copywriting skills working for different companies in the marketing field, even before getting my Master in Marketing & Communication.
