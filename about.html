Studies

Department of "Foreign Languages and Cultures"
University of Perugia

Here is where I got my Bachelor's Degree in Foreign Languages and Cultures. The courses were mostly focused on the study of the languages and the literatures of the corresponding cultures.Through these courses, I was able to improve my knowledge of the cultures I was studying and my mastery of the languages. It is also where I started to deeply focus on the matters of linguistics and translation.

Department of "Languages, Comparative Literatures and Intercultural Translation"
University of Perugia

What I've started on the path to my first degree continued here. At the same University, I got my Master's degree in Languages, Comparative Literatures and Intercultural Translation by focusing way more on the comparisons between different cultures, which is a fundamental part of translation itself. As Umberto Eco would have said, translation is "saying almost the same thing" and this is only possible by knowing the cultures we are referring to.

Master in "Marketing, Communication & Made in Italy"
Comunicare l'Impresa

This is my latest accomplishment as a student. I was offered the possibility to get this Master for being one of the best performing students in 2020. This really helped me to better understand the features of marketing, commerce and e-commerce, via the lessons and conferences of important figures in these fields. I was even invited at the Parliament in Rome to receive a certificate to testify this accomplishment.

Jobs

Translator
Fiverr and Upwork

I've occasionally worked as a translator through the online platforms Fiverr and Upwork for a few years, now. I've done enough jobs to create a nice net of contacts so that, when they need something, they know who to look for.

This job really helped me to improve my English and especially to understand more hardware-related matters when it comes to Information Technology.

Receptionist
